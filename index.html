About company

  • Smart & Unique Designs

With over two decades of excellence, Theyyampattil Furniture has become a trusted name in the world of premium custom-made furniture across the UAE. Established in 2000, our journey has been built on a foundation of craftsmanship, quality, and customer trust. We operate 3 elegant showrooms in Dubai, Sharjah, and Abu Dhabi, supported by two large-scale production facilities and a dedicated team of 300+ skilled professionals. Every piece of furniture is designed, manufactured, and installed completely in-house — ensuring full control over quality, materials, and finishes.
